# Claim 2 limitations and deviations

universal conclusion comes from the finite-state proof derivation, not
extrapolation from 23 epsilon values, the bounded search, or `p_1000`.
Reported numerical constants are specific to the two-state instance. The
derivation constructs one sufficient alpha and does not claim it is minimal.
This is an independent executable proof audit, not a proof-assistant
formalization.
